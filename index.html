    Some other things you should know is that integrating a function is like doing the opposite of derivate. For example the derivative of 2x is 2.
    The integral of 2 is 2x so there you see that you are just reversing the fact that you did the derivative. Another example is 4x^2. The derivate of 4x^2 is 8x.
    Therefore, the integral of 8x is 4x^2 because it is like you are undoing the derivative. Some other cool tips to know is what finding the second derivative does.
